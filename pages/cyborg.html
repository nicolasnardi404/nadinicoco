cyber bodies

fisk and nadi nicoco

Our performance is a sonic and visual representation of the clash between the queer body and machines. By blending adolescence with maturity in poetry, performance and music, we explore possibilities of the body and mind through gender expression.

Fisk is a queer artist based in Berlin. She seeks to explore the realms of club music with experimentation in genres, styles and deconstructed beats.

nadi nicoco is a brazilian artist. they work in different media investigating the tensions between the limits and possibilities of the body as a self-experimentation machine.
